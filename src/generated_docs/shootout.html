LOCAL PREVIEW. These numbers were measured on a developer workstation (and a --quick throughput pass), not the graviton reference runner — directional only. Capability and behavior checkmarks are environment-independent and final. Canonical numbers come from a dispatched perf_results/shootout/ run.
