What you're looking at

A real <fsl-instance> hosting <fsl-viz> and <fsl-editor> in its slots. The instance owns the split layout and the draggable gutter; editing writes back to it, the machine rebuilds, and the graph follows. No glue code.

Try this

  • Completions — Ctrl+Space after a shape: or color:
  • Color chips — change crimson to teal and watch the graph + chip follow
  • Lint — break a line (e.g. delete a ;) and watch the underline + status
  • Theme — toggle ☾ Dark; the editor re-themes live
  • Toggles — turn Chips / Lint / Complete off and on

Drag the gutter

  • Resize the panes; double-click the gutter to reset to 50/50. ▤ Stack flips to a vertical split.
